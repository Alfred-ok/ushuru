About Us

Empowering members through smart, secure, and sustainable investments since 2013.

Ushuru Investment Co-operative Society Ltd (UIC) is a member-owned investment society established to empower individuals through sustainable and affordable land and property investments. Since its inception in 2013, UIC has grown into a trusted investment partner, enabling members to achieve financial stability, ownership, and long-term wealth creation.

Our Vision

To be the investment co-operative of choice in Kenya and beyond, offering sustainable and trustworthy wealth creation opportunities for all members.

Our Mission

To provide diverse, affordable, and profitable investment options through professionalism, transparency, and member-driven initiatives that promote financial growth and stability.

Core Values

  • Integrity — We uphold honesty and fairness in all our operations.
  • Transparency — Our members stay informed and involved.
  • Reliability — We deliver on our promises.
  • Prudence — We invest responsibly to protect member wealth.
  • Empowerment — We help members make informed financial decisions.
